About Us

Hole in One Golf Resort, nestled in the heart of serene landscapes of Portugal, is a premier destination for golf and luxury. Established with a passion for the sport and hospitality, our resort offers world-class golf courses, including the renowned Sea View course, alongside exceptional accommodations and dining. Our mission is to provide unforgettable experiences, blending championship golf with unparalleled comfort and personalized service.

With a commitment to excellence, we cater to golf enthusiasts, tourists, and corporate groups, offering tailored packages and activities to suit every need. Discover the ultimate golfing getaway at Hole in One.
